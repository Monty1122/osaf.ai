Open Sovereign AI Federation

Building the open foundation for sovereign AI.

OSAF — the Open Sovereign AI Federation — is a vendor-neutral, non-profit association advancing sovereign, independent and interoperable AI infrastructure worldwide.

Apply to join Learn more View press release
Sovereignty is not isolation. It is choice, resilience and control. OSAF gives members a neutral, competition-law-compliant framework to collaborate on open standards, reference architectures, governance practices and policy engagement while remaining commercially independent.

Mission

A neutral forum for sovereign AI infrastructure.

AI infrastructure is becoming critical national infrastructure. OSAF exists to reduce concentration risk and promote a trusted ecosystem of sovereign AI operators, infrastructure providers, researchers, public-sector bodies and enterprise users.

What OSAF does

  • ✓Develops open technical standards, APIs and reference architectures for sovereign AI deployments.
  • ✓Creates governance and assurance frameworks for sovereignty, security, compliance and trust.
  • ✓Supports knowledge sharing between members through working groups, publications and events.
  • ✓Advocates for policies that support independent compute infrastructure and open AI markets.

Workstreams

Four practical areas of collaboration.

OSAF is built for practical, outcome-oriented collaboration across technical standards, governance, capability building and policy engagement.

01

Open Standards

Interoperability specifications, deployment patterns, APIs and reference architectures that reduce lock-in.

02

Governance & Trust

Assurance frameworks for data residency, operational control, security and transparent AI operations.

03

Capacity Building

Technical working groups, shared learnings, benchmarks and operating practices for sovereign AI providers.

04

Policy Advocacy

Engagement with government and regulators to support resilient, competitive and sovereign AI ecosystems without coordinating member pricing, customers or markets.

Membership

For organisations building the sovereign AI ecosystem.

Membership is open to qualifying AI operators, infrastructure providers, research institutions, government agencies and enterprises committed to independent AI capability.

◆

Full Members

Organisations operating or deploying sovereign AI infrastructure and participating in standards, governance and working groups.

◇

Associate Members

Organisations onboarding into the ecosystem or contributing expertise, software, policy or research capacity.

○

Observer Members

Government, academic or institutional observers participating in selected events and policy discussions.
